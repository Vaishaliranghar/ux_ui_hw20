Problem Statement

Young health-conscious professional workers need to efficiently plan diverse and nutritious meals for themselves because their busy schedules often lead to repetitive and less healthy meal choices which negatively impact their overall well-being.

We have observed through user research that a busy schedule increases the challenges of healthy meal planning. Users are less likely to watch “how-to videos” or try recipes that require an investment of time.

Users need an easy and fast way to put together healthy recipes. How might we develop a recipe app that is convenient and time efficient for users?

Success will be measured based on the decreased time it takes to prepare meals.
